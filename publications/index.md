<p>My research focused on computer architecture, specifically on how silicon interposer technology could enable new multi-chip processor designs. Most of this work was done during my MASc at the University of Toronto in collaboration with <a href="https://research.ece.ncsu.edu/arclab/" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Prof. Natalie Enright Jerger</a> and <a href="https://researcher.watson.ibm.com/researcher/view.php?person=us-loh" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Gabriel Loh</a> at AMD Research.</p>
